The Specimen Cabinet

Eighteen pinned entries, catalogued in the order they were discovered. Hover to disturb them. Click if you dare.

Zainab — kutti, kamini, macchar, saand, gendi, lendi, shemdi, kaan-ka-mail, bagal-ka-paseena, dandruff-of-questionable-origin, hair-in-my-food, bathroom-drain, cat-poop, buffalo, burden-on-this-earth, spider, cockroach, cricket — and somehow, through all of it, the most disgusting creature I have ever known.

Nobody else on earth gets called this many disgusting things and comes out the other side more loved, not less. That's kind of the whole point of best friends — nobody else has earned the right to call you a cockroach and mean it as the highest compliment.

Here's to 19. May the roasting never stop, may the friendship never break, and may you keep being the worst, filthiest, most shittiest specimen your best friend has ever had the joy of knowing.

— your best friend, curator of this cabinet since day one
